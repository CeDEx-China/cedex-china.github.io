CeDEx Lab studies how people make decisions through controlled experiments.

## Why Join

- Participation directly supports scientific research
- Most sessions are engaging and typically last under one hour
- Participants receive payment for their time, with potential additional earnings based on choices and outcomes

## Who Can Participate

All current UNNC students are welcome. No specific academic background or year-of-study restriction is required.

## How to Register

1. Register on ORSEE: [http://orsee.nottingham.edu.cn/public/participant_create.php](http://orsee.nottingham.edu.cn/public/participant_create.php)
2. Use your official UNNC email address (ending with @nottingham.edu.cn)
3. Activate your account through the confirmation email
4. Enable invitations for both laboratory and internet experiments

## Important Notes

- ORSEE access is limited to the UNNC teaching area (campus desktop or eduroam connection)
- All studies obtain ethics approval before participation
- Data are used anonymously for scientific research
- Registration does not commit you to any specific study and you can deregister at any time

For volunteer-related questions, contact [CedexChina@nottingham.edu.cn](mailto:CedexChina@nottingham.edu.cn).
