CeDEx China focuses on fundamental and applied work in behavioural and experimental economics, with strong links to economic theory.

## Core Areas

- Behavioural and Experimental Economics
- Microeconomic Theory
- Game Theory
- Mathematical Economics
- Economic Psychology
- Interdisciplinary applications with AI, big data, and machine learning

## Research Strategy

- Identify and model human decision-making with theory and empirical evidence
- Test decision behaviour using laboratory and online experiments
- Build policy-relevant insights through rigorous, reproducible methods
